## Rating Scale Explanation

**1-10 Scale:**
- **1-3:** Poor
- **4-5:** Below Average
- **6-7:** Good
- **8-9:** Excellent
- **10:** Outstanding

---

## Required vs Optional Fields

### **Required Fields (*):**
1. Customer Name
2. Ambience Rating
3. Management & Service Rating
4. Food Quality Rating
5. How did you hear about us?
6. Overall Experience Rating

### **Optional Fields:**
1. Which dishes did you try?
2. Additional Comments

## Data Collected

For each review, you'll have:
- Customer's name
- 4 separate ratings (Ambience, Management, Food, Overall)
- List of dishes they tried
- Marketing channel (how they found you)
- Any additional feedback

This gives you comprehensive insights into:
- What's working well
- What needs improvement
- Which dishes are popular
- Which marketing channels are effective
- Overall customer satisfaction
